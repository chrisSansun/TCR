Email sign-ups. When you join the list or download the Mini Reset, we collect your name and email address. Kit stores your name and email address on servers in the United States. By subscribing, you consent to this transfer. Kit is certified under applicable data transfer frameworks. We do not share your email address with any third party for marketing purposes.

Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _gid) for site usage statistics.
  • Cloudflare cookies for security and performance.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies won't affect your ability to use this site.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. If you subscribed to our email list and want your data removed, unsubscribe via any email we've sent you, or contact us directly and we'll remove you manually.

If you're based in the UK or EEA, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

We retain your email address and name for as long as you remain subscribed. When you unsubscribe, Kit removes your data from our active list. Analytics data is retained for 26 months in accordance with Google Analytics defaults.
